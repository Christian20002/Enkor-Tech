Who are we?

We're ENKOR-TECH

ENKOR-TECH is a company that has their origins in Juárez City, Chihuahua, México. Our main focus has always been to provide maintenance services and offer repairing to cooling machinery in local and external areas of the country. ENKOR-TECH shows a clear and solid goal of effective work and high quality, sharing the expected dedication of a trusted service.

History

Our company was established on February 28th, on the year 2000 with the name "Enkor Multiservicios Industriales". We started to offer our services to the assembly plant industry, mainly on lab areas and in the facilities department.



Mision

To provide quality products and services to our clients and/or users that constantly satisfy their necessities and expectations. Through a countinuous improvement process in our administrative processes and human capital by constant training, we strive to reach lidership on the preventive maintenance and services field on a regional level.

Vision

Creation of a working team compromised with the company to achieve client satisfaction, maintaining constant self-improvement goals. Be the best on the preventive and corrective maintenance service field based on our values of intet=grity, honesty and respect.
