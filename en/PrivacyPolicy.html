How can you access, rectify, or cancel your personal data, object to its use, or exercise the revocation of consent?

You have the right to know what personal data we have about you, what we use it for, and the conditions of its use (Access). Likewise, it is your right to request the correction of your personal information if it is outdated, inaccurate, or incomplete (Rectification); to have it deleted from our records or databases if you believe it is not being used properly (Cancellation); and to object to the use of your personal data for specific purposes (Objection). These rights are known as ARCO rights.

To exercise any of the ARCO rights, you must send a request via email to servicio@enkortech.com and it must include:

  • Full name of the data subject.
  • Address.
  • Phone number.
  • Email address used on this website.
  • A copy of an official ID attached.
  • Subject line: “ARCO Rights”

Describe the purpose of the request, which may include but is not limited to: Revocation of consent to process your personal data; and/or Notification of the misuse of your personal data; and/or Exercise of your ARCO rights, with a clear and precise description of the data you wish to Access, Rectify, Cancel, or Object to. In the case of data Rectification, you must indicate the exact change and attach supporting documentation. It's important to note that in the case of revocation of consent, we may not always be able to fulfill your request or stop the use immediately, as we may be legally required to continue processing your personal data. Also, for certain purposes, revoking your consent may mean we can no longer provide you with the service you requested or that our relationship with you will end.
