WHat does ENKOR-TECH do?

ENKOR-TECH is a company that provides services for cooling machinery. Our focus is the preventive and corrective maintenance to equipment of thermal cycles machinery with their different variants, such as: Humidity, Altitude, Thermal shock, between others.

ENKOR-TECH not only is limited to what described in the upper section, but also works in electric design, equipment controlers updates and general efficiency improvement.

Fast response

With several capable team members and with transportation options, we can provide a service very close to its contract deal.

More over, ENKOR-TECH makes sure that a low response time to contact attempts is maintained, achieving a simple and effective agreement with clients.



Quality work

Our team has high level of preparations to carry out assignments for complex machinery and for high level difficulty tool handling.

More over, communication is kept on a clean and professional status, making sure regulations are satisfyingly fulfilled and general cleanliness in the work area is mantained.

Presence in Mexico

Our main office building and workplace is found in Juárez City, Chihuahua, which lets us respond to clients locally, and in the northern section of the country.

At the moment we have a totally equipped storage facility in Querétaro and in Mexico city, letting us give emergency service to centric and southern areas of the country.



ENKOR-TECH is proud of its high technical capacity. Thanks to this, we can present a great capacity within the regrigeration and electromechanic industry. Having extensive experience and knowledge of multiple techniques of thermodynamic , we are presented an opportunity to manage a high ammount of machines with different specifications. Working with us is a chance you cannot miss.
